Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • dash of salt
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 beaten eggs
  • 3 mashed bananas
  • 1 c broken walnuts (optional)

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350.

Lightly grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.

Mix flour, baking soda, and salt in one bowl. In another bowl mix butter and brown sugar. Stir in eggs and bananas. Mix the two bowls.

Pour batter over pan.

Bake for 60-65 minutes.

Let cool and enjoy!.
